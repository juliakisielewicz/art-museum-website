Make a gift

By making a donation to the Hertford Art Museum, you become an active member of the museum community and help us create new experiences.

You can decide for yourself whether your donation will go to the general activities of the museum, or to the creation of new temporary exhibitions, conservation of the collection or educational projects.

The Herford Art Museum is grateful for, and delighted with, every donation!

We find your support particularly useful for acquisitions, restorations, research, exhibitions and educational programmes.
