Gascoyne Way, Hertford SG14 1LH, United Kingdom

Visit for learning groups

The Hertford Art Museum is open for schools, universities, families and friends groups for guided visits of the current exhibitions.

Guided tours for learning groups must be reserved at least two weeks prior to your visit date. Any cancelation must be announced one week before. For any booking/cancelation matters contact booking office.

Pricing

Guided tours to the exhibitions cost:

  • £8 reduced ticket - only for groups listed here.
  • £12 - regual ticket (Hertford City Card does not applay for guided tours)
Teachers and tutors are free with ratio 1 tutor per 10 members of group.
